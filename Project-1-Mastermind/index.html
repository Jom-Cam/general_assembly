The Wise Wizard Gandalf has set you out on a task to get the ring to Mount Doom.
You must find the correct character in the right order to get you through the perelous journey.

Playing:
1. Press 'Start A New Game'
2. Choose the characters you think should go on the journey by clicking on them.
3. Once 4 characters are selected you must press the 'Submit' button.
4. The wise Wizard will let you know if you have chosen wisely
5. 'Gandalf the Grey' will show if you have the correct character but is not in the correct spot.
6. 'Gandalf the White' will show himself if you have the correct character in the Correct spot.
7. You have 10 Attempts before the rings falls to the hands of the Witch King!

Setting up Player vs Player
1. Player 1 sets up the character they want in the order they want shown in preview bar.
2. Player 1 then changes the opponent tab to Player vs Player then hits the Clear All Button
3. Player 2 now has to find the character required to get to Mount Doom.
